# <img src="figs/teachmaster_logo.png" alt="TeachMaster logo" width="52" align="center"> TeachMaster: Generative Teaching via Code

TeachMaster is a code-centric multi-agent framework for **Generative Teaching**: an intent-driven paradigm where educators act as high-level directors while AI agents handle the production of curriculum-ready teaching materials.

Instead of generating educational videos as opaque pixel-level outputs, TeachMaster uses executable code as an intermediate semantic medium. This makes the generated content interpretable, editable, and easier to control, while preserving the structure, pacing, and visual clarity required for high-quality teaching.

## Overview

High-quality online courses are expensive and slow to produce. Traditional course production requires repeated manual work across lesson planning, visual design, animation, narration, editing, and revision. TeachMaster addresses this bottleneck by transforming lecture outlines and pedagogical objectives into complete multimodal teaching videos.

The framework decomposes educational video creation into a collaborative agent workflow:

- **Content Planning**: converts raw teaching intents into coherent manuscripts and page-level instructional blueprints.
- **Presentation Generation**: turns each blueprint into code-driven visual animations, narration scripts, and synthesized speech.
- **Quality Validation**: improves execution reliability, audio-visual synchronization, and layout quality through iterative debugging and refinement.
- **Human-in-the-loop Editing**: supports natural-language refinement and direct code-level intervention for educator control.

## Key Features

- **Generative Teaching Paradigm**: shifts educators from manual content creators to pedagogical directors.
- **Code as Semantic Medium**: enables transparent, modular, and editable educational video generation.
- **Multi-agent Production Pipeline**: coordinates planning, visual synthesis, narration, TTS, debugging, synchronization, and layout optimization.
- **Cross-modal Alignment**: maintains consistency between visual events, spoken narration, and instructional logic.
- **Bilingual and Cross-disciplinary Support**: demonstrates strong applicability across Chinese and English materials and diverse subjects including AI, mathematics, biology, chemistry, linguistics, and physics.
- **Scalable Deployment**: supports large-scale course production with substantially reduced time and cost.

## Results

Experiments show that TeachMaster approaches the quality of human-crafted educational videos while offering a major efficiency advantage over traditional production workflows and end-to-end video generation baselines.

In the paper, TeachMaster achieves strong performance in:

- video generation quality,
- educational script quality,
- cross-modal semantic alignment,
- production efficiency,
- real-world educator and student feedback.

In practical deployment, TeachMaster has served over 1,000 educators and generated more than 30,000 minutes of educational content across over 40 disciplines. Producing a standard 45-hour semester-long course costs approximately **0.3%** of traditional online course production expenses.
